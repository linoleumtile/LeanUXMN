About

Lean UX MN began in March 2013 after attending Lean Day in NYC.

Meetup organizers Joe Williams (@woejilliams), Erik Mitchell (@mitc0185) and Beth McKeever (@linoleumtile) wanted to bring learnings about Lean UX to the Minnesota community, as well as expose other lean efforts in the area.
